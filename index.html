Biggest Mistake which Developers do:

What many students/developers make mistake is that they keep on reading tutorials one by one, and do not implement any of them. This way of learning is totally wrong and futile. Students should read one tutorial then implement what the tutorial has taught them, in their own code. This will make them understand the code correctly, otherwise I guarantee they will forget what they just read within the next 30 minutes or before.
